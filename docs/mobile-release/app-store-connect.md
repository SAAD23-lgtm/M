## Description

`Riq Store turns the bottled-water shopping flow into a mobile-native experience. Browse the catalog, compare brands and offers, save your cart between sessions, confirm the delivery address on the map, and complete payment through the current secure checkout path.

Key features:
- Product browsing and brand discovery
- Offer bundles for quicker ordering
- Persistent cart across app sessions
- Address confirmation with device location and map pinning
- Direct contact through WhatsApp, phone, and email
- Secure payment handoff to the existing checkout bridge`
