## Short description

## Full description

`Riq Store brings the bottled-water shopping flow to Android in a fast native experience. Browse products, compare brands, review offers, save your cart, pin the delivery address on the map, and complete payment through the current secure checkout bridge.

What you can do:
- Explore the full bottled-water catalog
- Browse brands and offer bundles
- Save your cart locally and continue later
- Use map-based location to confirm the delivery address
- Open WhatsApp, phone, and email support directly from the app
- Complete checkout through the existing secure payment flow

Riq Store is built to reduce friction for repeat ordering, mobile browsing, and faster order completion.`
